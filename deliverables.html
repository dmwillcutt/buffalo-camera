Cutting Edge Surveillance Technology

The bison will be observed by a combination of stationary cameras and an autonomous drone swarm using high definition video, revolutionary night vision lenses and thermal sensing optics. Due to breakthroughs in proprietary battery technology, the drone swarm will be able to stay aloft for weeks at a time. As a optional measure of security against poachers, Class III drones can be armed with non-lethal short range crowd control and deterrent devices including electroshock weaponry, electric nets, and belt-fed rubber bullet miniguns. (Note: Probability of a runaway "Berserker" event is statistically insignificant)
